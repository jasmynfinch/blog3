Delicious Strawberry Mango Smoothie

This is a delicious smoothie that combines the sweetness of strawberries and mangos.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 ripe Mango
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 3 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Wash strawberries.
  2. Peel and slice the Mango.
  3. In a blender, add the strawberries, Mango, milk, honey, and a few ice cubes.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
